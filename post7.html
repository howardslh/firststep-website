Breaking Into Investment Banking

Key Takeaways

Investment banking means 80-100 hour weeks, but also $150K+ starting salaries at age 22. What top banks actually want: technical skills (DCF modeling, LBO analysis, comparable company analysis), fit and polish in interviews, deal awareness (read the Financial Times daily), and relentless hustle -- networking matters more than GPA once you clear 3.5.

Your Hong Kong playbook: Target bulge bracket banks and elite boutiques. Leverage Asia's booming deal flow as your competitive edge. Drill 400+ technical interview questions until the answers are second nature. Aim for 3 coffee chats per week with bankers. And start early -- apply at least 12 months before your target start date. The recruiting timeline waits for no one.
