About

Bridging the gap between
Wall Street & Main Street.

First Step is founded by a finance professional with 8+ years of experience spanning buy-side portfolio management, sell-side research, and equity analysis across the Asia Pacific region.

Over the past 3 years, we have coached 50+ students and helped them land roles through sell-side, buy-side, and management trainee interviews at leading financial institutions.

Our content is available in English, Cantonese, and Mandarin - designed for young professionals and aspiring investors across Asia.
